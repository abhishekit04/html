Amity University Patna

Amity University, Patna is a private university that was established in the year 2017 by the Amity Education Group. It is located in Patna, Bihar, and is approved by UGC. This institute offers Undergraduate & Postgraduate courses in the field of Commerce, Computer Science, Engineering, Management, Law, English Literature & Journalism. It has state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities.
